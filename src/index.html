All American

This married couple ‚Äì they share the last name ‚Äì had 503 transactions between each other.

They own a dog and a car which they prefer to fuel at Chevron almost every two weeks. They really like pizza, their favorite is at Shakey's, occasionally they also eat Asian and German food.

And they are paying a loan - 20 transactions last year to be exact, usually paid Thursday, Friday or Saturday. It's always the same person making payments to the other, not always in full and also not constistently at the beginning or the end of the month. 3 transactions are charges, meaning the person who pays the loan requests money - loan refund.

Our married couple is a great example to show how your transactions can reveal a lot of things about you, including some life patterns. By far the most transactions are for grocery stores ‚Äì 25 x Walmart, 19 x Albertsons and 15 x CostCo. They went to Aldi and Walgreens a few times. They go shopping more than once a week, almost never on Wednesday or Sunday.

Looking at all transactions, I believe that the both of them live in Orange County.

There are 8 transactions including the term SDG&E. San Diego Gas & Electric (SDG&E) "provides natural gas and electricity to San Diego County and southern Orange County in southwestern California". They visited a theater in the region (Regal Eastvale), "Chicago Ribs" appears 5 times (could be a reference to "Chicago for Ribs" restaurant that has 3 locations (Los Angeles, San Pedro and Redondo Beach), LAX, the airport in Los Angeles, is mentioned twice about one month apart. Other transactions that support this assumption include the In n out, Aldi (within California only in the south), and "Banfield Pet Hospital".
